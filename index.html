What this is for

Small fixes, not a big rebuild.

I check for the simple things that make customers hesitate: missing info, broken links, confusing contact routes, or key details that are hard to use on a phone.

People can’t find the basics

Menus, services, prices, opening hours, location, service areas or key details are hidden, outdated or hard to read.

They can’t take the next step

The call, book, order, quote or enquiry route is unclear, broken, buried too low down or awkward on mobile.

Your online details don’t match

Website, Google, Facebook, Instagram or booking/menu links tell different stories, which makes the business look less reliable.

You stay in control.

The first check uses public links only. If a fix needs access later, it should be done through proper platform permissions, not passwords sent over message.

No passwords by form, email, Instagram, Facebook or text.

No risky changes: I do not casually touch payments, ordering systems, booking systems, plugins or anything that could break the business.

Quick answers

Common questions.

Do I need to give you access?

Not for the free check. I start with public links. If we agree a fix that needs access, I’ll ask you to add my business email with the lowest useful permission. Please do not send passwords.

Do you guarantee more customers?

No. I can make obvious customer-facing issues clearer, but I cannot guarantee sales, rankings or enquiries.

What if someone else manages my website?

That’s fine. I can give you plain-English notes to send them instead of trying to take over.

What is not included?

Full website rebuilds, SEO campaigns, paid ads, online ordering systems, payment systems, complex booking setups, logo design and social media management.
