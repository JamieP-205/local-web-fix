## 1. Mobile homepage clarity
- What to check: Can a customer understand what the business does in 5 seconds?
- Bad sign: vague headline, important info buried, hard to read on phone.
- Simple fix: clearer top section, main action buttons, shorter wording.

## 2. Click-to-call
- What to check: Does the phone number work as a tap-to-call link?
- Bad sign: number inside an image or plain text only.
- Simple fix: add `tel:` link or clear call button.

## 3. Contact/enquiry path
- What to check: Can a customer enquire, book, order or ask a question easily?
- Bad sign: hidden form, broken email link, unclear next step.
- Simple fix: clearer button, working email link, simple form.

## 4. Opening hours
- What to check: Are hours clear and consistent across website, Google and social pages?
- Bad sign: missing hours, old festive hours, mismatch with Google.
- Simple fix: update hours section and recommend Google update.

## 5. Menu/services clarity
- What to check: Is the menu or service list easy to find and read on mobile?
- Bad sign: blurry image, broken PDF, outdated price list.
- Simple fix: clearer menu button, readable text, updated link.

## 6. Google Business Profile basics
- What to check: business name, category, hours, website link, phone, photos, description.
- Bad sign: old photos, broken website, missing category/description.
- Simple fix: improvement checklist or simple updates if access is available.

## 7. Photos and trust
- What to check: Are photos current and useful?
- Bad sign: no photos, poor quality, old branding.
- Simple fix: recommend 3 - 5 current photos or reorder visible assets.

## 8. Reviews/review path
- What to check: Can customers see reviews or leave one easily?
- Bad sign: no review link or unclear social proof.
- Simple fix: add link to Google reviews where appropriate.

## 9. Social links
- What to check: Do Instagram/Facebook links go to current active pages?
- Bad sign: dead accounts, old page, wrong platform.
- Simple fix: update or remove broken links.

## 10. Speed/accessibility/basic UX
- What to check: readable text, tap targets, contrast, obvious buttons, no broken layout.
- Bad sign: tiny text, buttons too close, slow heavy page, poor contrast.
- Simple fix: simple CSS/content/layout improvements.
